MEQA.MY

Welcome to MEQA.MY, where we embrace the essence of modesty with a touch of boldness. Founded in 2022, MEQA.MY is a local Muslimah brand dedicated to offering a wide range of stylish and comfortable apparel, including tops, bottoms, sets, dresses, kaftans, kurung, abayas, and hijabs. Our mission is to empower women to express their individuality with confidence, blending simplicity and elegance in every piece. At MEQA.MY, we believe in being basic but bold, and we strive to provide you with timeless fashion that reflects your unique style.p>
